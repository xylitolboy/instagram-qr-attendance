HOW IT WORKS

SETUP IN
4 STEPS

1

Run the setup wizard

Paste the backend script into Google Apps Script, deploy as a Web App, then paste the URL into setup.html. Takes about 5 minutes.

2

Open admin before each event

Enter session number + date. Click Generate. Download the QR PNG.

3

Display the QR at your event

Attendees scan with their phone, enter their Instagram ID, tap Check In.

4

Sheet updates in real time

New guests get a row. Returning guests get their count incremented and the session date marked O.
